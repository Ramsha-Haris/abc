ABOUT ME

As a Trainer at Omni Academy, I instruct Frontend Web Development course that includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and an introduction to React.js. My goal is to equip students with a solid understanding of these key technologies and prepare them for success in the web development industry.

Previously, I served as a Temporary Visiting Faculty at Institute of Space Technology , where I taught GIS Programming. In this role, I developed comprehensive course materials, including syllabi, lecture notes, assignments, and assessments. I engaged students through interactive discussions, hands-on exercises, and practical applications of GIS software, including customization and tool development using ModelBuilder and Python scripting.

I graduated from the University of Karachi with a B.S. in Software Engineering and earned my M.S. in Remote Sensing and Geographic Information Science from the Institute of Space Technology. During my graduate studies, I worked as a Research Assistant at the US-Pakistan Center of Advanced Studies in Water at Mehran University of Engineering and Technology, Jamshoro, Pakistan. In this role, I contributed to the Indus River Water Level Monitoring project in Sindh, utilizing satellite radar altimetry. My responsibilities included processing, analyzing, and visualizing geospatial and altimetric data.

I am dedicated to advancing my career in remote sensing, GIS, and Earth observation. My research interests focus on land cover change, terrestrial and coastal water management, and the impacts of climate change. I am committed to enhancing my programming skills and utilizing cutting-edge technology to deepen our understanding of environmental issues and address the challenges posed by climate change.
